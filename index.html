Your body after 40 is not the same body as before.

The frustrating part is that the way you used to lose weight — eating less, grinding through cardio, white-knuckling it — that stuff doesn't just work less now. For a lot of women in perimenopause, it actually makes things worse.

Here's what most people miss:
  • ⚡Hormone shifts in perimenopause change how your body burns fat — sometimes for years.
  • 😴Sleep loss and chronic stress physically block fat loss at the cellular level.
  • 🚫Eating less often backfires — it slows your metabolism down even more.
  • 💪Your body now responds to specific kinds of training that didn't matter in your 30s.
